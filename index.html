Dalgona Coffee Recipe Page

Dalgona coffee is a Korean coffee recipe, which was very popular during the lockdown. When people got bored, they wanted an entertainment and an adventure, for which they tried this recipe. To their surprise, it turned out well and here we are, making a recipe page for the legendary coffee.

Ingredients

  • Instant coffee powder
  • Hot water
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Making...

Step 1: Add coffee powder and hot water
Take a bowl of hot water. Add 4 tabelspoons of coffee powder and 2 tablespoons of sugar. Keep the mixture ready.
Step 2: Stir!!!
Use a hand held mixer, to stir the mixture well. Mix it till the whole solution turns fluffy or creamy.
Step 3: Add milk and enjoy
Add lukewarm hot milk to the fluffy coffee mixture and serve it with a spoon and a straw.
